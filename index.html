About The Event

• In coming years, there won't be a single industry in the world untouched by Artificial Intelligence(AI) . Industries like business, health, and others are all subjected to AI. A transformative force, you can either choose to understand it now or loose out on a wave of incredible change, but why stay in the dark any longer? #AL2019 is bringing to you all you need to know about AI and how you can take advantage of it on your working industry.

•All these transformations and advancements are setting up the tone for the future- a future which will be powered by “SuperAI”

• This June, TeleSoft via TeleSoftAI is bringing together students from College of Informatics and Virtual Education to share and experience the power of Artificial Intelligence.

•The event aims on giving awareness to students and know where and how we can utilize this technology to solve different problems.

Event Sessions

INTRODUCTION TO AI

By Meshack Bernard

In the introduction session some questions like :- What is Artificial Intelligence?
Is AI a real Science?
Can a computer ever really be smarter than human being? Will be answered .

PHILOSOPHY OF AI

By John Fedinandi

If you need to get answers in questions like :-
Philosophy of AI itself?
Can a computer “think”?
Can a computer have free will?
Can a computer be conscious?
Can a computer feel?
This session will provide answers to those questions. .

FRONTIERS OF AI

By Filbert Daniel

This session will explain the following:-
The main research areas of AI.
What is robotics?
What is computer vision?
What is speech recognition?
What is natural language processing?

IMPACT OF AI ON HUMAN LABOUR

By Zephania Reuben

Obviously, technological improvement have raised productivity and increased economic output throughout human history. In this session will be exploring different perspectives on technological improvement specifically AI and how its impacts on human labour.

AI in SECURITY

By Briane Lyimo

Artificial Intelligence is changing the game of cybersecurity, analysing massive quantities of risk data to speed response times and augment under-resourced security operation. In this session will analyse how we can apply Artificial Intelligence to improve the security in information technology and other aspect of human life.

AI in HEALTH

By Ramadhani Ramadhan

Health sector is one of the important sectors , knowing that AI has a lot to do with this sector. One of AI’s biggest potential benefits is to help people stay heathy so they don’t need a doctor, or at least not as often. Then , come and get know how AI and the Internet of Medical Things (IoMT) in combination are already helping people.

AI in NETWORKS

By Salehe Said

Next generation network analytics driven by Artificial Intelligence and machine learning promise to revolutionize conventional infrastructure, management models ,simplifying operations , reducing cost , and providing fresh insight. This session will provide a very clear insight on the application of AI in the communication filed.

AI in BUSINESS

By Ramadhan Massawe

The artificial Intelligence and Business Strategy initiative explores the growing use of artificial intelligence in the business landscape. The exploration looks specifically at how AI is affecting the development and execution of strategy in organization. Then, how we can utilize the power of Artificial Intelligence to our business? this session have answers to this question.

DEEPFAKES

By Zephania Reuben

DeepFakes offers the ability to swap one face for another in an image or a video. The new breakthrough is that, using deep learning techniques , anybody with powerful GPU, and training data, can create believable fake video and audios. In this session we will explore in depth this technology, its advantages and its potential of misuse.
